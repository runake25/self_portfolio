The film trusts silence more than explanation, and that restraint gives it emotional weight.

## What stood out

- Framing repeatedly places characters near edges, reinforcing distance.
- Dialogue is simple, but pauses carry subtext.
- The ending lands because it avoids an over-explained resolution.

## Takeaway

Great storytelling often comes from what is omitted, not what is added.
